Final Logo Designs

My logo is a typographical design inspired by my initials “AB.” Initially, I wanted to design a descriptive logo, but after some feedback, I decided I wanted my name to be front and center because my name is who I am. I decided to use a complementary color scheme with the help of Adobe Color. I chose blue (#757BBB) first because it was one of my favorite colors and represents reliability and responsibility. Then, I chose yellow (#EDD577) to complete the complementary color scheme and incorporate some contrast into the logo design. This page contains multiple logo versions, including a Primary Logo, a Primary Logo with a Name, Black and White, and a Favicon.
