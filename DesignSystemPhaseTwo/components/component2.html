Use Cases

  • Ensure the footer is placed on the bottom of all pages.
  • Ensure the logos link to the appropriate communication channels (Email and LinkedIn).

Accessibility Considerations

The footer should be accessible using the keyboard to allow screen reader usage. Add alt text to the footer logos (email and LinkedIn) to communicate they are links to other sites. Keep the navigation bar background color #EFECE4 and the text color #000000 to ensure that the WCAG construct ratio between the background and text is 17.78.1.
